Privacy Policy

We respect your privacy and keep your documents on your device.

Overview

PDF Tools Suite is designed to run entirely in your browser. We do not upload, transmit, or store your files on any server. All operations happen locally using client‑side JavaScript libraries.

Data Processing

  • Local only: Files are processed in memory within your browser.
  • No server upload: The application does not send files to any backend.
  • Temporary data: Intermediate data is cleared when you refresh or close the tab.

Service Worker & Caching

We use a Service Worker to cache application assets (HTML, CSS, JS) for performance and offline use. Your documents are not cached by the Service Worker.

Cookies & Analytics

We do not use tracking cookies. If analytics are added in the future, they will be anonymized and disclosed here.

Third‑Party Libraries

We rely on open‑source libraries such as pdf-lib and pdf.js to perform PDF operations locally. These libraries run client‑side and do not transmit your data.

Your Rights

Since we do not collect personal data or store documents, there is no data to access, delete, or export. For any questions, please contact the maintainer listed in README.md.

Updates

This policy may change as the application evolves. Significant changes will be reflected on this page.
